THE BOYS
How It Should Have Ended

A fan rewrite — from The Seven Hall to final credits
Written by Sandy
Screenplay by Claude
Contains Full Spoilers
Genuinely disappointed with how episode 8 wrapped up — so I wrote my own ending. Full rewrite from the Seven Hall scene all the way to post-credits.

The screenplay format was written with the help of Claude — I gave it my vision scene by scene and it shaped it into this. All the ideas are mine, Claude just made it readable.
INT.
The Seven Hall — Dawn

Butcher stands alone facing the sunrise. Vial in hand. The city below him, quiet for once.

Hughie walks in from behind.

Hughie Don't do it Billy. There are kids out there. Normal people who just had V in their blood and never asked for any of this. You release that — you're killing all of them too.

Butcher doesn't flinch.

Butcher Already made my peace with that.
Hughie Then I'm stopping you.

They fight. No powers, no tricks — just two broken men who know each other too well. Hughie catches him across the jaw. Butcher throws him into a wall. They crash through glass together and hit the floor hard.

Both lay there. Breathing heavy. Sun pouring in through the windows.

They pull themselves up. Face each other. Neither swings.

Hughie's eyes are wet.

Hughie Please.

Long silence.

Butcher looks at the vial. Looks at Hughie. And smiles. Bloody teeth, busted lip.

Butcher Oi.

He throws it.

SLOW MOTION — the vial tumbling toward the marble floor. Hughie dives. Fingers outstretched.

Too late.

It shatters.

Camera zooms into Butcher's face. Still smiling.

MONTAGE
News Broadcasts — Worldwide

Supes dropping mid-flight. Falling from buildings. One had a civilian pinned by the throat — his grip goes slack, he collapses convulsing on the pavement. The man stumbles away gasping.

Chaos everywhere. Some people cheering. Some people screaming. Nobody knows how to feel yet.

Godolkin University. Students on the grass. Some not moving.

EXT.
Somewhere in the City — Gen V Kids

Marie, Jordan, and Emma are together when it starts.

Jordan doubles over first. No warning. Just a sharp gasp and then they're on their knees.

Marie grabs them.

Marie Jordan — hey, what's—

Then she sees it. The boils spreading across Jordan's face and neck, skin bubbling and splitting, black blood starting to trail from the corner of their mouth. Emma grabs the wall behind her, legs giving out, the lesions crawling up her arms.

Emma starts vomiting. Dark black blood.

Marie spins between them, completely helpless, not understanding what she's watching.

Marie What is happening — someone help — HELP—

She reaches for Jordan with both hands and freezes.

Her own hands.

The same boils. Starting at her fingers, creeping up her wrists. She turns them over slowly like she doesn't believe what she's seeing.

Then the blood hits her throat.

She collapses beside them.

Three of them on the floor.

INT.
Brooklyn Safehouse

The TV is on. Every channel. Supes dropping worldwide.

MM stares at the screen. Then at Annie. Then back at the screen.

MM Motherfucking Butcher.

Nobody argues. Nobody's surprised. They just stand there for a second letting it land.

Annie So what do we do now?
MM We do what we can. Get the V out of everyone we can reach. Starting here.

Kimiko steps toward Annie without being asked. She already knows. Annie nods.

Kimiko takes her face in both hands and lets the blast go — controlled, precise, frying the V out. Annie's glow surges brilliant white, then cuts out completely.

Silence.

Annie looks at her hands. Empty. Human.

Annie Where's Ryan?
MM (quietly) He's gone. Left before all this. Don't know where.

Annie absorbs that. Nods slowly.

Then — Kimiko drops.

No warning. She hits the floor and the virus moves fast, spreading across her face, her neck. Lesions start appearing. She starts hyperventilating.

Annie is on her knees beside her instantly.

Annie Hey. Hey, look at me. You're okay. You're going to be okay—

MM stands over them. Fists clenched. Jaw tight.

MM (low, shaking) Goddamn it Butcher.
KIMIKO'S POV — Annie above her, eyes wet, repeating it's going to be alright like if she says it enough times it'll be true. MM behind her, cursing Butcher under his breath, the anger on his face the only way he knows how to grieve.

She looks at both of them. Takes them in one last time.
Kimiko (barely a whisper, tears in her eyes) I'm going to meet Frenchie.

Her eyes close.

Annie's hand finds hers and holds it.

POST CREDITS SCENE
Post Credits
INT.
Vought Tower — Sub-Level

Lights flickering. Alarms dying out somewhere above. The building half abandoned — guards gone, supes down.

Butcher walks alone through the corridor.

He finds it.

Soldier Boy's cryo-chamber. Untouched. Right where they left him. Frozen, unchanged, completely unaware the world just flipped upside down without him.

Butcher stares at it for a moment. Then smiles.

Butcher (shouting) Oi cunts.

Footsteps from behind. MM and Hughie appear on his left. Annie on his right.

Nobody says anything. They just stand there together, looking at the chamber.

CAMERA SLOWLY ZOOMS OUT — the four of them still, the chamber behind the glass.

Then it PUSHES IN on Soldier Boy's face. Eyes closed. Still.

A beat. Nothing moves.

Same shot. Same framing.

His face slowly becomes Kimiko's.

Eyes closed. Sealed chamber. White walls. Oxygen tube. No wounds.

A beat.

Her eyes open.
